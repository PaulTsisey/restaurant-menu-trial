Welcome to Sankofa Afro-Continental

We are a restaurant that specializes in serving delicious and authentic African dishes and some continental dishes. We use fresh quality ingredients, and we prepare our dishes with care and passion. Wether you are looking for a hearty meal, a light snack, or a refreshing drink, we have something for you.

Our Menu

Here are some of our popular dishes you can try:

  • Jollof Rice: Served with salad, plantain and meat or fish. 45 cedis
  • Egusi soup: Served with fufu or eba. 60 cedis
  • Red red: Served with fried plantains, egg and gari. 35 cedis
  • Bobotie: Served with yellow rice. 35 cedis
  • Piri piri chicken: Served with matapa. 45 cedis
  • Bunny chow 20 cedis
  • Lasagna: Served with garlic bread and salad. 70 cedis

We also have a variety of drinks such as coffe, tea, juice, soda, beer and wine. You can also try our desserts such as blaklava, malva pudding, kheer, and ice cream.

About Us

Sankofa Afro-Continental was founded in 2022 by a group of friends who wanted to share their love for African food with the world. We have passion for cooking and sharing our culture with our customers. We are proud to offer our diverse and flavourful menu, and we are always happy to serve up new and exciting dishes.
